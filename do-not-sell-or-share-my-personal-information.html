Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

California residents have the right to opt out of sharing their personal information with third parties in a way that may have been considered a “sale” under the California Consumer Protection Act (CCPA) and is now considered “sharing” under the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA). To learn about our personal information processing activities including third party sharing, please review our Privacy Statement.

What This Means

Softeq does not sell your personal information for money. However, we may share certain personal information (such as cookies and usage data) with our marketing and analytics partners to improve your experience, personalize content, and measure the effectiveness of our campaigns. This kind of sharing may be considered a “sale” or “sharing” under California law.

Your Rights as a California Resident

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

1. Opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information.

2. Know what personal information we collect and how we use it.

3. Access, correct, or delete your personal data.

4. Limit the use of sensitive personal information, where applicable.


How to Opt Out

To exercise your right to opt out, please choose one of the following options:

1. Use the Global Privacy Control (GPC)

If your browser supports the Global Privacy Control, we honor it as a valid opt-out request.

2. Email Us

You may also contact us directly at service@handchoice.com with your request.
