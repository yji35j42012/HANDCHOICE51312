Solutions for Digital Retail

At HAND CHOICE VENTURES, we help retailers adapt to the challenges of eCommerce with end-to-end software development. Our solutions support inventory automation, prevent stock-outs, and enhance customer engagement to build lasting loyalty

Get in Touch for Details

Why Go Digital?

Digital transformation in retail is driven by evolving customer expectations. Shoppers now research and compare products online before buying, expect mobile payments and digital coupons in-store, and frequently share their experiences on social media afterward.

To keep pace, brick-and-mortar retailers must deliver seamless, personalized shopping experiences across multiple touchpoints, including:

Mobile-assisted shopping
90% Customers increasingly rely on smartphones to guide their in-store purchases.
Omnichannel experience
73% Buyers expect a consistent journey across online, mobile, and physical channels.
Personalization
71% Tailored offers and experiences build stronger customer loyalty.
Digital retail integration
84% The line between offline and online shopping is fading, making unified experiences essential

Smart Retail Solutions to Transform Your Business

Digital Signage

At HAND CHOICE VENTURES, we design custom digital signage solutions that help retailers deliver promotional content, guide in-store navigation, and engage customers more effectively.
Our capabilities include:

  • Multimedia streaming for digital displays
  • Mobile and web apps for signage content management
  • Interactive signage enhanced with Augmented Reality features

Proximity Marketing

At HAND CHOICE VENTURES, we create location-aware solutions that help retailers engage customers and boost sales by delivering personalized notifications directly to smartphones.
Our expertise includes:

  • End-to-end proximity systems covering sensors, firmware, back-end, and mobile applications
  • Retail intelligence platforms for analyzing customer behavior and optimizing marketing strategies

Inventory Management

At HAND CHOICE VENTURES, we help retailers optimize supply and demand while reducing shrinkage through smart inventory management solutions. By leveraging sensors, RFID tags, and beacons, we enable real-time tracking of inventory location, availability, and storage conditions.
Our expertise includes:

  • RFID- and beacon-powered inventory tracking systems
  • Inventory management software integrated with ERP, CRM, and WMS platforms
  • Predictive analytics powered by machine learning, combining historical and real-time sales data

How Can We Help?

Boosting Customer Loyalty

We digitalize customer loyalty programs — and that’s what 95% of your loyalty program members want. Our mobile development team builds native and cross-platform customer loyalty apps with multi-level rewards systems, social features, live chat functionality, and chatbots.

Changing Customers’ Reality

Besides beacons and digital signage, we’ll help you transform in-store customer experience with Augmented Reality. To that end, we develop marker-based catalog apps, AR navigation systems driving foot traffic to your store, and virtual try-on solutions.
