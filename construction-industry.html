Digital Solutions for the Construction Industry

At HAND CHOICE VENTURES, we support engineering and construction companies in modernizing operations and improving efficiency. Our services include re-engineering legacy back-office systems, automating site inspections with digital tools, and using data from equipment sensors to reduce downtime and extend asset lifespan

Why Go Digital?

The construction industry has been slow to adopt new technology — due to limited R&D budgets, inefficient knowledge transfer, weak project monitoring, and the unique nature of construction sites. 

By not implementing specific hardware and software solutions at scale, construction businesses are missing out on an opportunity to complete projects faster, ensure a safer working environment, comply with industry regulations, optimize operational expenses, and refine waste management practices.

Fostering Innovation in Construction

Collaboration and Supply Chain Management

We help construction companies modernize IT systems and migrate to the cloud, enabling unified access for subcontractors, suppliers, and clients. Our expertise includes:

  • Integrating enterprise applications such as digital asset management, procurement, accounting, and building information modeling (BIM)
  • Migrating and modernizing legacy applications in the cloud
  • Enabling access to critical data via web, desktop, mobile, and wearable apps
  • Enhancing data security with blockchain-based smart contracts

Equipment Maintenance

To anticipate equipment failure and optimize fleet performance, we design IoT-driven maintenance solutions. Our services cover:

  • End-to-end sensor data capture, storage, and analytics on cloud-based platforms
  • Applying machine learning models for predictive maintenance
  • Visualizing historical and real-time insights through IoT dashboards and BI tools

Construction Site Inspections

We support digital inspection solutions that help generate site maps and track progress remotely. Our expertise spans:

  • Computer vision for video analysis and object recognition
  • Augmented reality for image labeling and 3D visualization
  • Dynamic reporting through modern BI and analytics platforms

Green Construction and Sustainable Building

We help companies reduce energy consumption and adopt sustainable practices with intelligent building technologies. Our capabilities include:

  • Integrated control systems: HVAC, air quality, smart lighting, and thermostats
  • Building automation and predictive management powered by AI and cloud platforms
  • Advanced construction methods including modular approaches and 3D printing

How Can We Help?

Engaging Workers in Digital Construction

One of the biggest challenges in construction digitalization is overcoming outdated practices. At HAND CHOICE VENTURES, we create AR and VR solutions that support workforce training and reduce onboarding time. These include remote guidance tools, AR-enabled equipment manuals, and apps that visualize building designs directly on site plans

Preventing Accidents on Site

We help construction companies strengthen safety with smart technologies. Our solutions include wearable devices, sensors, in-vehicle cameras, and radar systems that monitor worker location, detect hazardous areas, and provide tools for reporting unsafe conditions in real time
