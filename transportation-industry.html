Solutions We Deliver

Fleet Management

We develop mobile, web and desktop solutions to help transportation companies automate fleet management tasks, gain insights into fleet operations, and optimize their performance. In our portfolio we have:

  • Fleet management systems
  • Document management solutions (DMS)
  • Document capture platforms

Dealer Management Systems

Our custom-built dealer management solutions allow auto dealerships to manage their business on the go. Softeq’s systems facilitate:

  • Integration of CRM to process requests
  • Inventory management
  • Customer vehicle selection and customization tools
  • Real time communication with sales managers

Cargo Transportation

We provide a complete suite of freight exchange solutions that help transportation companies align supply, keep shipping records, handle freight data, and enable accurate information exchange among carriers, drivers and customers. Partner with us to get:

  • Systems for loading operations and delivery
  • Supply chain solutions
  • Freight management solutions

Apps for Valet Parking

We turn to mobile app development to devise valet parking solutions that allow customers to conveniently book and pay for valet services. Our solutions feature:

  • Locating the nearest valet parking services using Apple Maps
  • Obtaining information on available parking locations
  • GPS routing to a selected valet pickup location
  • Generating a QR code to check in and out of a parking area

Checking Flight Schedules and Airport Navigation

Our team is well-versed in delivering digital solutions for the aviation sector. These solutions enable seamless passenger experience and improve the quality of service at the airports, providing users with information on:

  • Flight schedules
  • Airport infrastructure
  • Local transportation
  • Car parking venues

Travel Planning Solutions

A specialist in the domain, HAND CHOICE has practical experience designing web services for travelers that help them organize information for pre-planned trips. The trip booking system allows customers to:

  • Organize travel information and documents into a single file
  • Assemble a travel packet: a route, a weather forecast, metro maps, hospital locations, embassy locations, gas stations
  • Upload itineraries
  • Upload copies of passports and driver’s licenses

Sustainable Transportation Solutions

Solutions we build address urban issues like pollution and traffic congestion. Also, we help transportation companies mitigate their part in global emissions. We believe that the future of transportation is in electric cars, sustainable public transport, carpools, and bikes. So we deliver:

  • Systems for electric cars that connect to traffic infrastructure
  • Fleet and freight management solutions reducing the carbon footprint
  • Apps for convenient public transit and green energy transport
  • Apps for car sharing and bike sharing services
